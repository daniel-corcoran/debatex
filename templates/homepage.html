Welcome to DebateX

Engage in Civil and Logical Political Debates

DebateX is a platform dedicated to fostering structured, logical discussions on political topics.
We believe in the power of open, respectful conversations to bridge gaps and promote understanding.

How It Works

1. Craft Your Arguments

Add premises as nodes to build your case. Represent claims or data points to create a solid foundation for your debate.

2. Connect the Dots

Establish relationships between nodes by linking premises that support or contradict each other, weaving a coherent and logical web of arguments.

3. Shape the Debate

Vote on premises and connections you agree with, driving consensus and promoting well-founded arguments within the community.

4. Refine the Discussion

Participate in the daily elimination of the 10% most downvoted nodes, helping to maintain the quality and focus of the debates.
